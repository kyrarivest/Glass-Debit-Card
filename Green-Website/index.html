The Green Project

The project involves work to implement recommendations developed during energy audits of 15 single-family woodframe houses built around 1900. Although not listed on the National Register of Historic Places, they appear eligible for the Register and have been documented in the State Register of Historic Places. The proposed energy-related improvements consist of attic and basement insulation, replacement of furnaces with high efficiency units, and window repair and/or replacement. Copies of the energy audits and State Register forms are enclosed.
